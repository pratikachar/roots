Roots Now
**Brand details**
Core Business
Roots Now is a hyper-local agri-tech and automated micro-logistics platform. They operate a decentralized network of automated, vertical hydroponic farms housed inside retrofitted shipping containers placed directly within urban neighborhoods. They sell a "Farm-to-App" subscription model delivering leafy greens, rare herbs, and microgreens to a user's doorstep within 30 minutes of being harvested.

Target Audience
The definitive ideal customer is the "Culinary Purist and Conscious Parent." These are health-conscious foodies, home chefs, and affluent parents aged 28–48 living in major metropolitan food deserts or concrete jungles. They care deeply about the nutritional degradation of grocery store produce that sits in trucks for weeks, and they are willing to pay a premium for unmatched freshness, zero pesticide exposure, and a truly localized supply chain.

Brand Voice and Personality
Tone: Vibrant, transparent, and radically local.

Personality: It has a crisp, energetic, and sensory-driven personality. It bridges the gap between a high-tech agricultural operation and a welcoming neighborhood farmers' market. The language is conversational, fresh, and urgent (emphasizing the "harvested 30 minutes ago" hook), making the customer feel deeply connected to the literal source of their food.
