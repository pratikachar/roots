The Problem

The nutritional decline of long-haul logistics.

The produce on standard grocery shelves is rarely fresh. Most commercial greens are harvested weeks early, treated with preservation gases, and shipped across continents in refrigerated trucks. By the time this food reaches your kitchen, up to fifty percent of its original vitamin density has dissolved, resulting in muted flavors, soft textures, and unknown chemical exposures.

1,500+mi Average distance US produce travels before purchase
14days Average cold storage time before your grocery run
50% Nutrient loss during transit and storage
The Solution

Radical proximity changes everything.

Roots Now eliminates the supply chain completely. We retrofit shipping containers into pristine, hyper-local hydroponic ecosystems placed right in your urban neighborhood. Because our indoor growing environments are completely sealed from external contaminants, we never use chemical pesticides or herbicides. You receive pure, peak-nutrient produce that is still living when it leaves the farm.

The Technology

Controlled environment agriculture for urban spaces.

We do not rely on unpredictable weather patterns or chemical soil additions. Our indoor modular farms utilize advanced hydroponic systems that feed water and organic mineral solutions directly to plant root structures. This automated precision allows our crops to grow at peak efficiency, utilizing ninety-five percent less water than traditional open-field agricultural models while occupying a fraction of the physical land footprint.

By constantly monitoring ambient humidity, temperature, and light spectrum frequencies, our growing systems create ideal seasonal conditions for every crop all year long. This absolute environmental control means our rare herbs and tender greens develop intense, natural flavor profiles and unparalleled structural crispness, completely free from outdoor industrial pollution or soil-borne diseases.

Our Core Mission

Ending urban food deserts.

To reintegrate clean, nutrient-dense food production directly into modern metropolitan centers. We aim to eliminate urban food deserts by establishing automated agricultural infrastructure within walking distance of every major neighborhood, restoring immediate access to living food.

Our Long-Term Vision

To construct completely resilient urban food networks that protect metropolitan areas from climate-driven supply chain disruptions. By transforming underutilized city spaces into highly productive agricultural centers, we want to help modern cities become self-sustaining ecosystems.
