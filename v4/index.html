The nutritional decline of long-haul logistics.

Your "fresh" salad has been on a truck for two weeks.

The produce on standard grocery shelves is rarely fresh. Most commercial greens are harvested weeks early, treated with preservation gases, and shipped across continents in refrigerated trucks. By the time this food reaches your kitchen, up to fifty percent of its original vitamin density has dissolved, resulting in muted flavors, soft textures, and unknown chemical exposures.

Radical proximity changes everything.

We eliminated the supply chain.

Roots Now eliminates the supply chain completely. We retrofit shipping containers into pristine, hyper-local hydroponic ecosystems placed right in your urban neighborhood. Because our indoor growing environments are completely sealed from external contaminants, we never use chemical pesticides or herbicides. You receive pure, peak-nutrient produce that is still living when it leaves the farm.
